Our team developed a comprehensive system that integrates historical data, market trends, and real-time information to inform decision-making.     Charter Communications

    Data Analyst, February 2020 – March 2022

    At Charter Communications, I contributed to a significant customer churn prediction and retention strategy project. I developed a sophisticated system to forecast potential customer departures across Charter's diverse service offerings, including internet, TV, mobile, and voice. I built a robust data pipeline integrating multiple sources such as CRM systems, network logs, and customer interaction records. Using T-SQL for data manipulation and analysis, I processed vast amounts of data to identify crucial factors influencing customer churn. I engineered complex features capturing customer behavior and interaction patterns, which fed into my machine learning models. I implemented an ensemble approach combining various algorithms to achieve high accuracy in predicting customer churn. The project also involved creating a real-time scoring system and integrating it with Charter's existing CRM platform, enabling immediate risk assessments during customer interactions. I developed interactive dashboards for executives to monitor key performance indicators and collaborated with the marketing team to implement data-driven, personalized retention campaigns. This comprehensive approach to using data and advanced analytics significantly enhanced Charter's ability to retain customers and improve overall satisfaction.

    O'Reilly Auto Parts

    Data Analyst, January 2019 – January 2020

    At O'Reilly Auto Parts, I contributed to a crucial inventory management optimization project aimed at enhancing prediction accuracy for part requirements across all stores. I developed a comprehensive data pipeline, integrating diverse sources such as sales records and warehouse systems. Utilizing Informatica for ETL processes, I consolidated and transformed data into Snowflake, creating a robust foundation for analysis. I conducted in-depth data analysis using Python and SAS, uncovering seasonal and location-based sales patterns for various auto parts. Based on these insights, I built forecasting models to predict future part demands, crucial for maintaining optimal stock levels and minimizing both stockouts and overstock situations. I created interactive Tableau dashboards to visualize key inventory metrics, providing managers with easily digestible, actionable insights. Throughout the project, I collaborated closely with IT and warehouse management teams, employing JIRA for effective project tracking and communication. This data-driven approach significantly improved inventory management efficiency, reduced stock-related issues, and enhanced overall customer service by ensuring better part availability. My work contributed to more informed decision-making processes, leading to optimized inventory costs and improved operational efficiency at O'Reilly Auto Parts.

Additionally, I developed automated reports using Python and Plotly, collaborated with stakeholders to define KPIs, and optimized database queries and dashboard performance, significantly enhancing decision-making efficiency for DMart's management.
